Project Documentation

Project Title: Website Development for ABC Company

The ABC Company website development project is aimed at creating a modern and responsive website to enhance the company's online presence. The site will include features such as product catalogs, customer reviews, and an interactive contact form.

Project Methodology

We are following the Agile methodology for this project to ensure flexibility and adaptability throughout the development process. Agile allows us to iterate on the project through short sprints, each lasting two weeks, to ensure timely delivery and client feedback integration.

  • Scrum Framework: Weekly stand-ups, sprint planning, and sprint reviews to ensure continuous progress and alignment with project goals.
  • Sprints: Development will occur in two-week sprints with a clear objective for each sprint.
  • Client Involvement: Frequent communication with the client to ensure requirements are met and priorities are aligned.

Project Process

The project is divided into several key phases:

  • 1. Requirement Gathering: Initial discussions with stakeholders to define project scope, user requirements, and business goals.
  • 2. Design Phase: Creation of wireframes and prototypes for the website design. Once approved, the UI/UX team will create the final design.
  • 3. Development Phase: The project will follow a modular approach to develop various sections such as the homepage, product catalog, and contact forms.
  • 4. Testing and QA: Testing will occur after each sprint, focusing on functionality, performance, and user experience. Bugs will be tracked and resolved.

Conclusion

This project aims to deliver a user-friendly, functional, and visually appealing website that meets the client's needs. The agile methodology ensures flexibility in adapting to new requirements, and the thorough process will ensure timely and successful completion of the project.
